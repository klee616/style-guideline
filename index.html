Logo and Variants

Pocket Pals feature two marmots together as its logo. The reason we chose marmots was because it's a friendly and cute animal making it approuchable to a younger audience. Furthermore, it also represents BC (where the Vancouver Island Marmots are found) and creates a local sense of pride. In addition, the VI marmots are an endangered species which relates to the purpose of the app.

We chose Heavitas for its primative simplistic shape as users would look at it and associate the shape language with learning blocks used in elementry school. Its boldness also makes it easy for users to read and recognise what the title is. Although blocky, it does have a certain roundness to the corners which makes it less intimidating and harsh.
